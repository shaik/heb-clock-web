Privacy Policy

Hebrew Clock — Last updated: April 3, 2026

Hebrew Clock is a home screen widget that displays the current time as a natural Hebrew spoken phrase. This privacy policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data collection

Hebrew Clock does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access and makes no network requests.

Permissions

The app uses no permissions beyond those required to function as a home screen widget (scheduling periodic updates via AlarmManager). No sensitive permissions are requested.

Local storage

User preferences (theme, font size, niqqud toggle) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

Third parties

Hebrew Clock does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or advertising networks.

Children

The app does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer via the GitHub repository at github.com/shaik/heb-clock-web.
