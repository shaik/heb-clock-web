Features

  • Updates every minute Precise alarm-driven updates, even when the screen wakes from sleep.
  • Dark & light themes Choose the look that fits your home screen.
  • Three font sizes Small, medium, or large — with optional compact prefix and suffix.
  • Niqqud (vowel marks) Toggle vowel diacritics on or off.
  • Tap to configure Tap the widget at any time to open the settings panel.
